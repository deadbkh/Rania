Your lips? They are my paradise, where I lose myself,

Between my breaths and yours, new worlds come alive.

There, I find serenity, I find life itself.
Every touch from you remolds my being,

a passion that makes me thirst for more, crave more.

for I am a man who knows how to treat his queen.
You are the miracle I have awaited,
the woman unlike any other.
With you, I live a passion I once thought was only a dream,

I promise to forever be with you and within you,

Now, and until the day I hold your hand at our wedding,
and even beyond, until my last breath on this earth,

Forever,
